In Botswana, the registration process differs depending on whether the entity is formed as a **Society** (such as a burial society or professional association) or a **Co-operative Society** (such as a SACCOS).

Under the **Societies Act of 2022**, the government has transitioned to a digital-first registration model to strengthen compliance and oversight.
 

## 1. Registering a Society (Burial, Social, Professional)

This process is managed by the **Department of Civil and National Registration**.

* **Eligibility:** Must have at least **20 members**. Religious societies now require a minimum of **150 members**.
* **Office Bearers:** At least **two-thirds** of the executive committee must reside in Botswana.
* **The Process:**
1. **Letter of Intent:** Submit a letter to the local Civil and National Registration office.
2. **Application (Form A):** Complete three copies of Form A, including a membership list and the societyâ€™s constitution.
3. **Security Clearance:** Office bearers must provide security clearance reports from law enforcement agents.
4. **Online Portal:** All new registrations and re-registrations are strictly completed through the government portal at **www.gov.bw**.


* **Fees:** BWP 500 for associations/churches and **BWP 250** for all other societies.
* **Duration:** Typically takes **14 days** to process.

## 2. Registering a SACCOS or Co-operative

This process is managed by the **Department for Co-operative Development** under the **Ministry of Entrepreneurship**.

* **Eligibility:**
* **Standard Co-operatives:** Minimum of **10 people** (aged 18+).
* **Financial Co-operatives (SACCOS):** Minimum of **20 people** (aged 18+).


* **The Process:**
1. **Education:** Visit a local Co-operatives office for information on co-operative principles and practice.
2. **Viability Report:** Submit a report in the prescribed form demonstrating the economic viability of the proposed co-operative.
3. **Application:** Submit an application for registration to the Director, signed by all founding members.
4. **Bye-Laws:** Submit the co-operative's bye-laws for certification.


* **Fees:** A flat registration fee of **BWP 20.00**.
* **Duration:** Approximately **14 working days**.

### Key Regulatory Differences

| Feature | Society (Burial/Social) | Co-operative (SACCOS) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Primary Law** | Societies Act, 2022 | Co-operative Societies Act, 2013 |
| **Min. Members** | 20 (General) / 150 (Religious) | 10 (General) / 20 (SACCOS) |
| **Regulator** | Registrar of Societies | Director for Co-operative Development |
| **Core Goal** | Social/Charitable/Religious | Member economic interests |
| **Renewal** | Annual (Valid for 12 months) | Indefinite (subject to compliance) |
